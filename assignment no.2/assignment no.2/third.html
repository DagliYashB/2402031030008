Our Story

CleanSpotter was founded in 2019 by two friends, John and Sarah, who wanted to make it easier for people to take action for the planet. They started by creating a simple app that helped users find nearby recycling centers, and quickly grew their community by adding new features like trash cleanups, tree planting events, and eco-friendly product recommendations. In just a few years, CleanSpotter has become one of the world's largest environmental platforms, with millions of members taking action across 195 countries.
